Quoted from Collins, J. B. 2002. From Tribes to Nation: The Making of France 500—799. Wadsworth, Toronto, p. 144.

“largely due to the work of Adam Smith (1723-90).” To be fair to Adam Smith, he did not think that society could be based entirely on self-interest. For example, in his book Theory of Moral Sentiments Smith discussed at length the importance of empathy in human affairs.

“by sacrificing one copy of itself in the defender bee it will produce three more copies in the new bees” Because each of new four bees shares three quarters of her genes with the sacrificial defender, on average there will be 0.75 x 4=3 copies of the altruistic gene among them.
