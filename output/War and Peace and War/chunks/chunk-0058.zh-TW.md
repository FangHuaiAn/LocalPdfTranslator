The Suebi 以 Maroboduus 為首的部落聯盟，名為一種聯盟而非單一部族。Tacitus wrote that “the Suebi, unlike the Chatti or Tencteri, do not constitute an individual tribe: They occupy the greater part of Germania, divided among the nations with names of their own, although all are called Suebi in common. It is characteristic of the tribe to dress their hair on the side and bind it up tight in a knot. This distinguishes the Suebi from the other Germani, and their free-born from their slaves.” The distinctive hairstyle of the Suebi 在無數羅馬的描繪中可以找到 (例如，在羅馬的Trajan柱上)。 它也出現在考古學家在沼澤地發現的一些屍體上。 其中保存最好的是在Schleswig-Holstein的Osterby發現的一位日耳曼戰士的頭顱。 髮髻是人如何利用外貌來宣告“我們”與“他們”之間的象徵性界限的一個很好的例子。事實上，名稱Suebi 本質上意味著“我們”（“屬於我們的群體”）的意思。 incidentally, the names of such modern nations as the Swedes and the Swiss have precisely the same origin. The name of the later Alamanni also expressed the same idea, but with a different means. Alamanni means “the (true, real) people,” clearly a variation on the “us” versus “them” theme. The origin of the name the Goths is more obscure, but some authorities think it simply meant “people.” The name for the other great confederation, the Franks, however, had a different logic: it means “the fierce,” “the brave.”

部落聯盟，如Suebi，在Maroboduus和Arminius時期仍然是一種脆弱的政治組織形式。 這些個體部族可以很容易地從一位領袖轉向另一位領袖。 例如，Langobards (後來在意大利歷史中扮演了如此重要的角色) 最初是Maroboduus的聯盟的一部分。 然而，在Teutoburg森林戰役後，隨著Arminius的名聲日益高漲，他們轉向了他的效忠。 此外，統治者的制度還沒有根植於德國文化中。 部族士兵對他們的戰爭領袖的皇室野心持懷疑態度，並且擔心賦予他們過多的權力，他們可能會用來壓迫平民。 Tacitus describes the process by which the tribal confederations fell apart and their leaders perished as follows.
