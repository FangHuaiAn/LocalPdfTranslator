also been based at George AFB since July 1964 until its move to Ubon
RTAFB in the summer of 1965. Udorn was the most northerly Thai base,
placing it closer to northern Laos and North Vietnam and reducing the
tanking requirement for F-104Cs. After initial successes in the escort role,
the eight Starfighters were joined by 12 more from the 435th TFS on
22 July. All arrived safely despite the exhaust gas temperature indicator
in F-104C 56-0898 periodically dropping to zero on the long over-water
flight.
