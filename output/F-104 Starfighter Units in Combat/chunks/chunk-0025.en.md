Budget-driven shortages of spares and ammunition combined with
little forward planning meant delays in achieving full readiness for many
of the ANG units, and there were some general personnel shortages
because one-eighth of the Guardsmen who were mobilised had chosen to
remain in their Stateside jobs. However, the presence of Starfighters was
a powerful air superiority deterrent, as it had been in Taiwan. They
demonstrated very fast reaction times and stellar acceleration, which made
them superior to all other available fighters in practice interceptions. This
was a major factor in ADC’s decision to re-admit some of the F-104As to
national control the following year.
Pilots adapted to the crowded European airspace environment and the
